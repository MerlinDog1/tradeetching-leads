# Trade Etching Direct — European Lead Research

## Project Overview
Research compiled for **Trade Etching Direct**, a UK-based chemical etching specialist (brass, copper, stainless steel) targeting the luxury hospitality, architectural, and signage markets.

## Research Objective
Identify B2B trade prospects and boutique competitors across continental Europe who:
- Work with brass/copper/stainless steel
- Serve luxury hotels, restaurants, architects, or high-end signage clients
- May offer chemical etching OR who could be potential outsourcing partners/joint ventures

**Strict Exclusions:**
- Standard trophy/key-cutting shops (Timpson-style)
- Printed plastics, acrylics, vinyl only
- Heavy industrial aerospace/medical/PCB etching (without decorative division)
- Pure laser marking without deep relief

## Methodology

### Search Strategy
1. **Web Search:** Bing search via web_fetch (only method working in this environment)
2. **Keywords Used (per region):**

| Region | Keywords |
|--------|----------|
| France | signalétique hôtel luxe, gravure laiton, enseigne laiton massif, plaque professionnelle laiton |
| Germany/Austria | Ätztechnik Messingschilder, Hotelschild aus Messing, Tiefgravur Messing, Architekturschilder |
| Italy | fotoincisione ottone, targhe albergo lusso, segnaletica ottone brunito |
| Spain | placas latón grabado, señalización hoteles lujo, grabado químico latón |
| Netherlands/Benelux | chemisch etsen messing, bewegwijzering hotel, messing gevelborden |
| Scandinavia | mässingsskyltar hotell, etsade skyltar, arkitektonisk skyltning |

3. **Company Verification:** Visited company websites to assess:
   - Manufacturing capabilities (laser, mechanical, chemical etching)
   - Material focus (metal types)
   - Hospitality/architectural client evidence
   - Contact information

### Research Limitations
- **Bing search heavily filtered** — many queries returned automotive results, unrelated forum content, or Chinese-language pages
- **French searches particularly problematic** — "gravure chimique laiton hotel France" returned Indonesian forum content (KASKUS)
- **Some domains inaccessible** — signanetique-inox.fr did not resolve
- **Contact emails rarely extractable** — most company sites use contact forms rather than publishing direct email addresses
- **Language barriers** — Italian/German/Spanish company sites difficult to parse for capabilities

## Findings Summary

### France
- **Graphitis** — Strong hotel signage focus. 40+ years. Digital print + laser. Not chemical etching but potential partnership.
- **Otypo** — Laser engraving focus. Grenoble-based. 20 employees. NOT chemical etching.
- **helloPRO** — B2B product platform with brass hotel plaque listing. Manufacturing method unknown.

### Germany
- **schilder.plus** — Online engraver. Laser + depth engraving on acrylic/aluminium/stainless. Consumer focus.
- **Stempel Komischke** — 50-year family company. Düsseldorf. Laser + paint + stainless steel. General signage.
- **Gravur-Fabrik** — Online shop. Laser + digital print. Consumer gifts focus.

### Italy / Spain / Netherlands / Scandinavia
- **No verified companies found** due to search engine filtering issues
- These regions require follow-up research with alternative search methods
